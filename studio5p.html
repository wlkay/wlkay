Studio 5 Preamble

Content, context, and concept, that was what this project entwined. A hyper creative solution needed to be found for the context of this project, as the site happened to be ISIS controlled Syrian countryside, and the client was homosexual fashion designer, Lee McQueen

The client and siting necessitated the concept - an actually defiant approach to the building. That approach draws inspiration from McQueen’s interest in the savage and the natural, and thus the Syrian Thistle, beautiful and defiant despite a harsh environment.

The exterior and interior dichotomy, that of harsh spiky exterior and soft foamy interior, reflect McQueen’s own personal struggles, while the program of the project continues and entwines McQueen with the theme of defiance, as it happens to be a night club. Since the club and the club goers probably would not last the night in ISIS territory, they all might as well go out in the most defiant way possible, joyful and dancing to rave music.
