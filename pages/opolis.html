project overview

Lead Product Designer and Product Steward for Opolis, a ground breaking new technology using blockchain to democratize employment - moving work from corporate-centric to network-centric - and giving workers the power and freedom to choose benefits for themselves.

PEO

An update to Opolis' onboarding process for new Employers to the PEO. The current system is a complicated, manual hodgepodge of third party applications, so the difficulty in starting this project was understanding all the detailed processes of our current system to make sense of what we needed to move forward, in addition to understanding the market for this type of project and Opolis' business needs.

I did this by establishing a design process that worked so well I have used it on other projects to similar success. What normally should have taken two months of research to discover requirements for this project only took two weeks, and we are currently in the development phase for this project. I implemented the open source tool by Pablo Stanley, Humaaans, as it elegantly fits into the organization and project. The goal is to hire a graphic designer to make custom illustrations for this project and for Opolis.
